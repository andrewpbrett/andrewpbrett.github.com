99 Things

Update 2 Feb 2016: Beyond 99 Things

In 2009 I bought a car in New York City and drove it to California to start a new job. I sold everything that didn't fit inside.

Since then, I haven't owned many things, deliberately. It's nice to fit everything into a car or similarly sized space. I thought it'd be fun to write them all down. Since deciding what counts as a "thing" that you "own" is a bit arbitrary, I used Nick Winter's rules as a guide, and also wound up with 99 Things.

There are no obnoxious affiliate links but you can hover over an item if you want to know more details.
