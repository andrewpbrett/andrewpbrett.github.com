Not only does this study go, as promised, beyond red and blue, but it provides a questionnaire (one page) that you can fill out to determine which group you would have fallen into had you taken the survey. The questionnaire itself is a little lacking, since you only have four choices for each question and some of the wording is dubious. But very worth a look.
